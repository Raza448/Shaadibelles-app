wedding stories/real weddings/sonia & vivek


Mar 23, 2015

A wedding celebration is perhaps marked by the splendor and richness of the food served. Whether you are planning an elaborate Indian wedding or one celebrating multiple faiths, the food that you choose to serve will be remembered long after your big day.

For over two decades, we at Moghul Caterers have been creating fabulous luxury wedding palates that has pleased hundreds of guests.

Our team of extraordinary chefs creates unique, inspired menus for all our clients. Whether it's North Indian, South Indian, Gujarati, Chinese, Far Eastern, Middle Eastern, Italian cuisines or a mix of many, our chefs leave no stone unturned to give you the very best of that cuisine.

Off-premise catering services, wedding co-ordination & partnership with renowned locations are services we provide throughout the United States, as well as in anywhere in the world.
